About Us

Butter & Bliss Bakery is a family-owned bakery that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We specialize in creating delicious baked goods that are made with love and care. Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality products that are made from scratch using only the finest ingredients. Whether you are looking for a sweet treat to enjoy with your morning coffee or a custom cake for a special occasion, we have something for everyone. Stop by our bakery today and experience the joy of Butter & Bliss Bakery!
